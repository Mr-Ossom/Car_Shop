BMW X6 M

The BMW X6 M is a high-performance Sports Activity Coupé (SAC) that blends the practicality of a SUV with the dynamic handling of a sports car. It features a powerful M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine, M-specific features, and advanced driving assistance systems. The X6 M is known for its aggressive styling, including a distinctive front fascia, horizontal kidney grille slats, and optional M Carbon mirror caps.

BMW M5 Competition

The BMW M5 Competition is a high-performance executive sedan that delivers a blend of comfort and extraordinary performance, making it a standout choice in its segment. It's known for its powerful engine, sporty handling, and luxurious interior.

BMW 5 Series

The BMW 5 Series is an executive car manufactured and marketed by BMW since 1972. It is the successor to the BMW New Class sedans and is currently in its eighth generation. The car is sold as either a sedan or, since 1991, a station wagon (marketed as "Touring"). A 5-door fastback (marketed as "Gran Turismo") was sold between 2009 and 2017.

BMW M8 Competition

The BMW M8 Competition is a high-performance, luxury grand tourer known for its powerful engine, sleek design, and advanced technology. It boasts a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine delivering 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, allowing for a 0-60 mph sprint in just 2.5 seconds. The M8 Competition features a unique blend of sporty and luxurious elements, including carbon fiber exterior accents, M-specific sports seats, and a digital cockpit.

BMW Hybrid PHEV

BMW's Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) combine a combustion engine and an electric motor, offering both electric-only driving and the range of a traditional gasoline-powered vehicle. These vehicles can be charged from external power sources like public charging stations or home charging. PHEVs can drive emissions-free and offer lower fuel consumption by regularly charging with electricity.

BMW X M

The BMW XM is a large, luxury, high-performance SUV and the first M-division model to offer a plug-in hybrid powertrain. It combines a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine with a powerful electric motor, offering up to 653 horsepower and 800 Nm of torque. The XM also features adaptive suspension, M Sport differential, and M xDrive all-wheel drive for a dynamic and sporty driving experience.
